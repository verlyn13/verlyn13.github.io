Research Philosophy

My approach combines mathematical rigor with practical implementation. Rather than purely theoretical work, I build systems that embody mathematical principles, allowing direct observation of their behavior and limitations. This methodology provides insights that inform both theoretical understanding and practical application.

Current Focus Areas

  • Multi-Agent Coordination: Developing algorithms for autonomous agent cooperation based on category-theoretic principles
  • Tool Integration: Exploring how AI systems can effectively integrate with existing computational tools
  • Mathematical Foundations: Applying formal methods to verify and understand AI system behavior
  • Practical Implementation: Building working systems that demonstrate theoretical concepts
